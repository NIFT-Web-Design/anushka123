Soothing herbal tea that restores deep sleep

Sleeping well is one of the most important activities that keep your body sane and healthy. Unfortunately, factors like stress, pain, chronic conditions, genetic diseases, and environmental situations can affect sleep easily. This blend is a cup full of warmth and calm to consume every night before bed. Drink this tea to achieve high-quality, deep sleep every night for more energised days.

Health Benefits

  • Chamomile soothes anxiety and stress through antioxidants
  • Valerian root reduces hormonal stress, especially during menstruation
  • Lavender encourages relaxation through aromatherapy

Tasting Notes

floral bitter minty mildly sweet

Ingredients

Valerian Root | Chamomile Flowers | Catnip Leaves | Gotu Kola Leaves | Mulberry Leaves | Lavender Flower | Shankhpushpi Leaves | Passion Flower | Green Tea

Brewing Instructions

  • Add 1 teaspoon of tea leaves to an empty pot
  • Add water to the bot and bring to a heat (do not boil)
  • Brew for 5 minutes
  • Preferred without milk
